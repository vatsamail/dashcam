# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Goal
REQ-PRD-001: Capture dashcam frames continuously and store locally with metadata.

## User stories
- REQ-PRD-002: As a user, I want reliable continuous recording with bounded frame loss.
- REQ-PRD-003: As an integrator, I want scriptable simulation and firmware flow.
- REQ-PRD-004: As a validation engineer, I want traceability from requirements to tests.
